A full-service contractor website designed to turn visitors into qualified leads with clear service paths, financing-first messaging, and strong credibility signals for homeowners and businesses.

### What was the project goal?
The objective was to create a lead-generation powerhouse for Koi Roofing and Solar. The site needed to establish local authority, explain complex financing options clearly, and convert residential and commercial property owners into active leads.

### How did we solve the challenges?
We focused on trust indicators and conversion pathways. Key solutions include:
- **Financing-First Messaging:** Clear explanation of solar savings and roofing financing options, helping customers make informed choices.
- **Interactive Lead Funnel:** Multi-step estimation forms that qualify leads by roofing type, solar capacity, and location.
- **Credibility and Trust Signals:** Prominent display of certifications, local reviews, safety standards, and project portfolios.
- **Ultra-Fast Speed:** Built on Astro to load media-heavy project galleries instantly on mobile devices.

### What key results were achieved?
- Substantial increase in high-quality local leads.
- Reduced bounce rates on financing pages due to improved clarity.
- Seamless responsive experience on smartphones and tablets.
