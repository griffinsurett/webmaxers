Fun-first design with online booking, tour details, and an atmosphere that sells the experience before guests ever step on board.

### What was the project goal?
The primary goal was to build a modern, high-converting booking platform for Sandy Hooker Tiki Tours. The client needed a website that communicates the fun, high-energy atmosphere of their Jersey Shore boat tours while simplifying the reservation process for guests.

### How did we solve the challenges?
We designed a responsive website that places the booking system front and center. Key solutions include:
- **Interactive Tour Scheduling:** Users can easily view available times and book their tiki boat experience in real time.
- **Atmospheric Branding:** Vibrant color schemes, custom iconography, and prominent video placements capture the excitement of the Atlantic Highlands harbor experience.
- **Location and Contact Clarity:** Direct integration of harbor details (Atlantic Highlands Municipal Harbor), contact information (201-441-1487), and clear directions.
- **Performance Optimization:** Built using Astro and React to ensure instantaneous page loads, which prevents potential drop-offs during booking.

### What key results were achieved?
- Improved booking conversion rates through an optimized reservation pipeline.
- Enhanced brand presence with a professional, fun, and modern design.
- Zero friction mobile booking experience for guests on the go.
