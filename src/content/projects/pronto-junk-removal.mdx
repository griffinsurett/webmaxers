A bold single-page experience that highlights service areas, rapid response scheduling, and social proof for a busy junk removal team.

### What was the project goal?
The goal was to design a high-impact, single-page website for Pronto Junk Removal. The company needed a bold online presence that showcases their junk hauling services in New Jersey and encourages immediate booking.

### How did we solve the challenges?
We built a streamlined, action-oriented landing page. Key solutions include:
- **Rapid Response Scheduling:** A prominent booking form that allows residential and commercial clients to request quick quotes.
- **Service Area Mapping:** Clear listing of New Jersey service areas to capture local search engine visibility.
- **Social Proof Integration:** Before and after galleries showing work results alongside real customer reviews.
- **Mobile First Navigation:** Fully responsive grid layout that allows customers to schedule a cleanup directly from their phones.

### What key results were achieved?
- Higher conversion rates on immediate junk removal requests.
- Strong local SEO visibility for key New Jersey service locations.
- Improved user engagement through interactive before and after sliders.
