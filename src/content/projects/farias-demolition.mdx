We paired bold typography with credential highlights to help this demolition contractor build trust with municipal and commercial buyers.

### What was the project goal?
Faria's Demolition required a website that matches the scale and professionalism of their industrial operations. The goal was to build a site that qualifies them for large commercial and municipal demolition contracts.

### How did we solve the challenges?
We focused on safety, scale, and professionalism. Key solutions include:
- **Safety Standard Showcases:** Prominent highlighting of safety certifications, environmental regulations compliance, and heavy machinery details.
- **Project Portfolio Gallery:** High-resolution case studies showing completed commercial, residential, and industrial demolition projects.
- **Rapid Quoting System:** Streamlined intake form for estimators and project managers to submit RFP documents.
- **Responsive Architecture:** Clean, grid-based layout optimized for construction managers viewing the site from field tablets.

### What key results were achieved?
- Increased bid requests from commercial developers and municipal planners.
- Professional presentation of safety and compliance documentation.
- Fast loading times for image-heavy project galleries.
